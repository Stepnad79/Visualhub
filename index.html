VH-Tripod

VH Tripod is a tripod that has pan & tilt mechanism. Besides the functionality of pan left to right and tilt upwards, it can also be remotely controlled & easy to use.

Get Started Watch Video

Why Use VH-Tripod?

VH-Tripod offer automated phone camera adjustments via a remote controller. This hands-off approach improves control and accuracy, allows camera operation from a distance, and makes positioning the camera as easy as touching a button.

VH - TRIPOD

What are the features of VHTripod?

  • 01 Pan Tilt Remote Control

    Supports direct setting via the buttons on the operation panel, and supports convenient operation via remote controller.

  • 02 Application Field

    Designed for most cellphones and action cameras, 500g maximum playload, works silently and smoothly.

  • 03 High Frequency Remote Control Pan Tilt Camera Head

    433MHZ high frequency remote control system, 20m control distance in outdoor without any latency.
